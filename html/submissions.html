THE NOR'EASTER HISTORICAL JOURNAL

Submissions

​The final publication of the journal will encompass 30 papers in total that we select, each no shorter than 2,000 words or about ten pages in length. Papers should be researched based, but may cover any part of history, world or U.S.

Papers must be in Chicago format, as opposed to MLA. For more details about formatting, visit www.owl.purdue.edu for assistance.

Papers should be converted into a PDF document and sent to glideg@portlandschools.org
